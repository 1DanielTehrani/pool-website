Why mine Monero?
After ASICs took over cryptocurrency mining, especially cryptocurrencies using sha256 algorithm are heading to centralization.Cryptocurrencies were created as a decentralized financial system, which no particular person or organization is responsible for it. So this ASIC centralization should be fixed. Monero is a cryptocurrency which is strongly against ASIC mining. For all GPU miners, using GPUs to mine cryptocurrencies are still profitable (depends on electricity bill) and also can contribute to the decentralization of mining.
About
 This is a mining pool specific for mining Monero. No registeration needed. Connect to out pool with a miner, and start mining. We are just trying to contribute to the network by distributing hashpower, for a more reliable and decentralized network. So the pool fee is set to 0%. Only your hashpower depends on how much you earn.
 You can use a third party mining software to connect to our pool. xmr-stack supports both gpu and cpu mining, so even if you don't have a gpu in your machine, it is possible to share your hashpower.
